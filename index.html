Offer Your Items

The Problem

Electronic waste is a growing problem worldwide, with millions of tons of e-waste generated annually. Improper disposal of these items can lead to environmental and health issues. By offering your used electronics for recycling, you can help reduce the impact of e-waste and contribute to a more sustainable future.

The Solution

By partnering with CleanEarth Recyclers, you can easily and responsibly dispose of your electronic waste. Our recycling centers use state-of-the-art technology to extract and reuse valuable materials from your old devices, minimizing the environmental impact. What Can You Offer?

  • Computers and laptops
  • Phones and tablets
  • TVs and monitors
  • Small appliances and electronics

How to Offer Items

  1. Wipe all data from devices
  2. Disassemble items if possible
  3. Find a local recycling center or schedule a pickup to donate your items
  4. Avoid throwing electronics in the trash as they contain hazardous materials
